Whisked Wonder

This is the love story of Courtney Rasmussen and her kitchenaid. To be more clear, she just likes to cook and bake. Courtney was born in California and raised in Arizona. She lives with her husband and two sons along with their three cats. Courtney loves to code by day and bake by night. Her signature dish is her famous Snickerdoodles! Her pride and joy in life (besides her marriage and her sons) is her cherry red Kitchenaid mixer. Stay awhile and browse some recipes for dinner and dessert on your table tonight!
