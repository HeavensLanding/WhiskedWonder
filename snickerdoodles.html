Snickerdoodles

Ingredients

  • 1C margarine or butter, half melted
  • 1 1/2C sugar
  • 2 eggs (ideally room temp)
  • 2 3/4C flour
  • 1 t baking soda
  • 1/4 t salt
  • cinnamon sugar, for rolling

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cream sugar and eggs together.
  3. Mix in margarine or butter until smooth.
  4. Combine baking soda, and salt until smooth again.
  5. Add flour 1 cup at a time until bulky.
  6. Scoop into one inch balls or with a chef scoop and roll in cinnamon sugar.
  7. Shake excess sugar lightly back into bowl.
  8. Place on cookie sheet and cook at 350 degrees for 13-16 minutes or edges have slightly crisped.

Extra Notes for Success

  • It's easiest if while the sugar and eggs are mixing, to prepare the margarine/butter in the microwave.
  • Cookies need to be ROLLED and NOT DUSTED in cinnamon sugar. Maximum cinnamon sugar is part of the secret.
  • Cookies will look doughy when they come out of the oven. They need to cool at least five minutes on the cookie tray before removing to a cooling rack. This sets them up the rest of the way and ensures crispy outside, chewy inside.
  • Your snickerdoodles will not taste as amazing as mine because I will take the ultimate secret to the bitter end, but this recipe will allow you to get pretty close
